Hyperfov

Tools for exploring the internet with greater speed and dexterity.

We browse the internet the same way we did in 1994. Hyperfov is a new way to move through it at the speed of curiosity.

Despite their rich, interlinked structure, websites can only be explored linearly.

Navigating around a website is a slow process. It takes time to find a link, click it, move to the next page, scan it, and repeat the process. We need a new way to browse that allows for exploration down multiple paths, preserving context over time.

A website's external links provide context and opportunity to explore, yet they're never seen as one collection.

Links are buried in content and obscured by vague hypertext. Yet they're the key to moving quickly between websites and finding new destinations. We need a new way to see where a website points to.
